    • Welcome To The The Soul of Yoga!

    Soul of Yoga is a home for those who seek a more spiritual Yoga experience. Our classes and trainings offer a heart-opening, consciousness expanding encounter. If you have a yearning to deepen your inner knowing, elevate your wellness, discover your purpose and meet like-minded individuals, the Soul of Yoga is calling you home.

    We are deeply committed to preserving and passing on the authentic, and profound wisdom across many ancient yoga teachings and lineages. We share the best of the physical yoga practices, science, medicine, history, and philosophy, with no shortage of sacred and magical mysticism.

    Through our partnerships with the world’s most knowledgeable teachers we empower students to become vessels for these timeless teachings, and to share the beauty of these practices with authenticity and integrity. As a growing collective we are making the world a kinder, more compassionate place.

    Our Mission

    1. To share the profound experience of yoga practice through classes, workshops and professional trainings,
    2. To kindle your unique passion, wisdom and gifts,
    3. To empower you to share your gifts with the world in your own beautiful way.

    Authenticity, Lineage & Philosophy

    It is said there are many paths, but one goal. Soul of Yoga holds welcome space for a wide array of teachings, philosophy and lineages. Each teacher you’ll experience at Soul of yoga has extensive experience in their field, but we adhere to no one single belief system or dogma. It is our intention to provide multiple authentic perspectives, so you can find your own resonate path and deepest truth.

    The Right Practice for You

    All of our offerings are guided by the most experienced and well-trained teachers in the world today. From the very physically challenging, to the most subtle, from the deeply spiritual, to scientifically ensured, we meet you where you are and offer you a path forward on your yogic journey. Whether you want to drench yourself in sweat, in spirit, or scientific study you’ll find a way to participate with passion at Soul of Yoga.

    Spiritual and Scientific

    Soul of Yoga is the place where Harvard meets Hogwarts! In recent years the therapeutic effectiveness of complimentary treatments such as yoga, breathwork, meditation, sound healing and energy healing has gained attention from the scientific community. These interventions have been proven to have measurable effects on our mind and emotional states as well as on a physiological level. As such Soul of Yoga’s faculty includes Western experts speaking to modern understanding, including geneticists, neuroscientists, psychologists, researchers, cardiac and oncology experts, and behavioral specialists. For all that modern science has come to understand about yogic science, there still remains teaching that is shrouded in mystery. At Soul of Yoga, we invite you to delve into these ancient mysteries and philosophies by studying with experienced teachers who are mystics, philosophers, authors and healers as they unveil the deep wisdom of subtle anatomy, Ayurveda, Sound Healing, Reiki and the ancient texts. We have the highest respect for both science and spirit and the Soul of Yoga shines in the convergence point

    World Renowned Faculty and Master Teachers

    Whether teaching asana, science or mysticism, Soul of Yoga’s faculty are renowned throughout the yoga community for their depth of knowledge and unwavering commitment to preserving the authentic lineage of yoga. Each has decades of experience, and in many cases have “wrote the book” on their area of presentation. The breadth and depth of the experience and perspective shared by our faculty is unparalleled. This combined wisdom is a seeker’s dream come true! Studying with master-level faculty is enlightening and inspiring, and also fosters graduates that are skilled practitioners, and true ambassadors for the authentic lineage and mystical depths of the yogic teachings
